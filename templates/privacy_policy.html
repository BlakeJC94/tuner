Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it, and how to delete your data if needed.

Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Your Spotify username
  • Your top artists (as retrieved from Spotify)
We do not collect history of who you've matched with or what playlists you have generated or saved.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Determine your taste in music.
  • Match your top artists and taste with other users with similar music taste.
  • Generate a playlist based on shared interests with other users, which can optionally be saved to your Spotify account.
No data will be used to train AI or machine learning models.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. All data is used solely for this app's functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your data indefinitely to enhance the app's functionality by allowing other users to match with your profile.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your data or deletion of your data. If you would like your data to be deleted, please click here to delete.
